The Hive is a growing planet of free tools. It builds what the community asks for. No more, no less.

The rules

No investors.
No ads.
No agenda.
Tools should be free. The ones here are.
Free at the base tier, forever.

How it works

You suggest engines. They get built. The planet grows.

Every cell on the planet is a tool. Live cells glow gold. The rest are coming. What gets built next is decided by what people ask for — not by a roadmap, not by a board, not by anyone's agenda.

Support

Optional support keeps the lights on. If a tool earns a place in your day, you can support it when you can. Not a subscription, not a paywall. Just an honest ask.
